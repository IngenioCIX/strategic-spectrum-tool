## Overview

This tool operationalizes a strategic framework developed to help agencies navigate the critical question: **Should we innovate boldly or refine what's working?**

During "in-between" economic periods (pre-recession, post-recession, or periods of uncertainty), fear-based decision-making often paralyzes both agencies and clients. This assessment provides a structured approach to strategic positioning through 8 key gauges.

## Features

- **8 Strategic Gauges Assessment**
  - Client goals and risk appetite
  - Target audience preferences
  - Brand values and identity
  - Market and competitive landscape
  - Customer loyalty and brand equity
  - Budget and resource constraints
  - Cultural and social context
  - Time constraints

## The Framework

### Strategic Deviation
Breaking from established norms to create disruption, innovation, and differentiation. Best suited for:
- High-risk tolerance contexts
- Oversaturated markets needing differentiation
- Brands positioned as innovators or disruptors
- Audiences craving novelty

### Strategic Deepening
Refining and enhancing existing strategies to optimize performance and build trust. Best suited for:
- Risk-averse environments
- High customer loyalty requiring protection
- Conservative markets (finance, healthcare)
- Limited budgets requiring cost-effectiveness
